About Me

Professional Background

I am a Research Associate at the University of Glasgow, specializing in Security and Privacy of AI. My work focuses on developing privacy-preserving AI-driven systems for healthcare applications. Currently, I contribute to the Biomedical AI lab, where I design differential privacy algorithms for security monitoring.

Academic Journey

I hold a Ph.D. in Cybersecurity (Machine Learning) from Robert Gordon University, where my thesis centered on Robust, Effective, and Efficient Machine Learning Algorithms for Cybersecurity monitoring. Prior to this, I completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science at UNISZA Malaysia, researching network resource allocation in a simulated environment.
